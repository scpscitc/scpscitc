Workshop: Join the Future with Robotics!

Dear SCPSC IT Club Members,

Are you ready to explore the thrilling world of Robotics? Whether you're a tech enthusiast or simply curious about how robots work, this is your chance to dive in!


✨ What’s Coming Up? We're launching a Basic Robotics Class designed to ignite your creativity and enhance your technical skills. From understanding the basics to engaging in hands-on projects, you’ll receive a comprehensive introduction to robotics. If you enjoy this demo class, we may offer more sessions based on your interest!

👨‍🏫 Learn from the Best! Our classes will be led by expert mentors— professionals who are passionate about robotics and eager to guide you on this exciting journey.


🔧 What You’ll Gain:
01. Foundational Knowledge: Grasp key concepts of robotics, including mechanics, electronics, and programming.
02. Hands-On Experience: Build and program your own robots using cutting-edge tools and technologies.
03. Collaborative Projects: Team up with fellow enthusiasts to solve challenges and create innovative solutions.
04. Career Insights: Receive valuable advice on pursuing a career in robotics and related fields.
05. Olympiad Participation: Standout performers will have the opportunity to represent our club in prestigious robotics Olympiads and competitions, showcasing your skills on a larger stage!


💻 What to Bring: All participants are requested to bring a laptop (not any other device). If you can’t bring a laptop, don’t worry—that’s not a problem!


📅 When & Where:
Date: Thursday, 05.09.2024
Time: 10:00 AM
Location: ICT Lab, Savar Cantonment Public College.


🎯 Who Should Join? - Everyone! Whether you’re a beginner or have some experience, our class is designed to accommodate all skill levels. Just bring your curiosity and passion, and we’ll take care of the rest.

<>
📢 Don’t Miss Out! - Spaces are limited, so be sure to secure your spot in this transformative class. If you enjoy the experience, we might offer additional sessions based on your feedback. Let’s build the future together!
